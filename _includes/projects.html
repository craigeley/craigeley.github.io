Projects

A very concise list of what's cooking.

Radio

I'm currently ACLS Public Fellow working for To the Best of Our Knowledge, a show produced by Wisconsin Public Radio. I'm working on digital, social, and broadcast content.

Teaching and Research

My research and teaching interests center around sound, technology, the arts, and the environment. I'm currently one of the organizers of the new working group on sound studies here at UW-Madison. I'm also on the editorial board of the ASAP/Journal.

Writing

I'm currently working on a book manuscript that traces the history of environmental recording practices in the United States and examines their impact in American culture.
