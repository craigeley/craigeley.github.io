## 2012 Smithsonian Fellowship - Posted
I couldn't be more happy to end the posting drought on this blog by announcing that I've been named a 2012 Smithsonian Predoctoral Fellow. I'll be working at the [Center for Folklife and Cultural Heritage][1] with Jeff Place, listening to a ton of old science records and generally beefing up my dissertation. I'm really looking forward to exploring DC--it's a city I haven't spent much time in but have heard great things about. And if you know someone with an apartment for rent...
